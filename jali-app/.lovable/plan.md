

# Jali.ai - AI-Powered Health for Mothers & Communities

A warm, compassionate landing page showcasing Jali.ai's mission to bring AI-powered healthcare access to underserved communities, with a focus on maternal and child health.

### 2. Hero Section
- Large, welcoming hero with a warm gradient or imagery background
- Compelling headline highlighting your mission (e.g., "AI-Powered Health for Every Mother, Every Child")
- Supporting text about reaching underserved communities
- Two call-to-action buttons (e.g., "Learn More" and "Partner With Us")

- 3-4 service cards showcasing what Joli.ai offers:

  - **Healthcare Access** - Connecting people to care
- Clean icons and brief descriptions for each service

### 4. Geographic Reach / Impact Map
- Visual world or regional map showing where Joli.ai operates
- Highlight key regions or countries served
- Optional: Animated pins or markers showing impact areas

### 5. Impact Numbers Section
- Simple but powerful statistics bar showing reach:
  - Communities served
  - Mothers helped
  - Health questions answered
- Displayed with animated counters for visual appeal

### 6. Footer
- Contact information (email, location)
- Social media links
- Quick navigation links
- Newsletter signup option
